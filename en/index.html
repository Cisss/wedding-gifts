Practical Information

What You Need to Know

  • Share your preferences: We'd love it if everyone, regardless of specific wishes, fills in the preferences form.

    Dietary requirements: Let us know in advance if you have any dietary wishes. We can work with anything — Francisca doesn't eat gluten, lactose, added sugars, peanuts, non-organic meat, soy... etc. We'd love to hear your wishes.

    Speeches: Would you like to give a speech at the wedding? Lovely! We'll figure out together how that fits in.

  • Parking: Parking is available at both locations. If you have any questions, please contact us. We're happy to help.
  • Public transport: Both locations are easily accessible by public transport.

    From Driebergen-Zeist station to Zeist City Hall
    Take bus 50 or 56 to stop Het Rond / 1e Dorpsstraat. Journey time is approximately 8 minutes. Buses run every 10 minutes.

    From Zeist City Hall to the party venue
    From Busstation Zeist (approximately 16 minutes' walk from the city hall), a bus runs to stop Beauforthuis, Austerlitz. Total journey time is approximately 33 minutes. Buses run every 15 minutes.

    From the party venue back to Driebergen-Zeist station
    From stop Beauforthuis, Austerlitz you can travel back to Driebergen-Zeist station in approximately 33 minutes. Connections are also available later in the evening.

    Tip: rent an OV-bike
    We recommend renting an OV-bike (public transport bicycle) from Driebergen-Zeist station. This is often the quickest and most pleasant way to travel between the venues.

    Please note: the party venue is located in the middle of the forest.

  • Accommodation: Don't live in the Netherlands and looking for a place to stay? Together we can always find a solution. Feel free to get in touch.
  • Cancellation: Can't make it after all? That's a shame, but nothing we can do about it. Just let us know in time.
